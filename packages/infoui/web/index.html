Server Information

Key shortcuts
USE YOUR PHONE GPS (K KEY) TO FIND LOCATIONS NOT ON THE MAP
F1 to pull up house menu near a house door
F2 to pull up animation wheel
F3 to access job menu if your job has one
F4 to access your inventory
F5 to pull out and use your ID
F8 to pull out your PC
M to open full map
G will access your GPS to set waypoints to locations
K to open your phone
U will unlock and lock a vehicle
CTRL + R in a vehicle to enable radio
SHIFT + R as a police officer to cuff the nearest player
B to pickup and drop gun
Commands
/lottery to open lottery menu
/want near a player as a police officer to make them wanted
/arrest as a cop to send a wanted player to jail
/rape to try to infect someone
/rob as a thief to pickpocket a player
/g or // to talk in global chat
/help to view this menu again
Server Rules / Laws
No random killing players
NOS vehicle mod is illegal
Speed limit in city = 50 km/h
Cultivating or processing drugs is illegal
Content
Consider starting a job to start making money.
Buy a car use it to drive for fun, for a job, with friends or as a bank heist get away vehicle.
Become a drug manufacturer to make some cash.
Race others players for money
Trade stocks by going to the Wealth bank on your GPS. Stock prices update every 10 minutes.
G will access your GPS to set waypoints to locations
Jobs
You can select a job by visiting the job manager downtown where you spawned in.
Most jobs will have job menu accessible by F3
You will receive a paycheque when you are logged, every 10 minutes
If your job provides a gun dont kill random people for fun.
Tip: Police officer is the highest paying job
