Server Information

Content
Consider starting a job to start making money.
Buy a car use it to drive for fun, with friends or as a bank heist get away vehicle.
Become a drug manufacturer to make some cash.
Race others players for money
Trade stocks by going to the Wealth bank on your GPS. Stock prices update every 10 minutes.
G will access your GPS to set waypoints to locations
Jobs
You can select a job by visiting the job manager downtown where you spawned in.
Most jobs will have job menu accessible by F3
You will receive a paycheque when you are logged, every 10 minutes
Key shortcuts
F1 to pull up house menu near a house door
F2 to pull up animation wheel
F3 to access job menu if your job has one
F4 to access your inventory
F5 to pull out and use your ID
G will access your GPS to set waypoints to locations
U will unlock and lock a vehicle
